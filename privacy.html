Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Koinonia is a Trello Power-Up for community fund allocation and group coordination. This policy explains what data we access and how we use it.

Data We Access

  • Trello board, list, and card data — only from boards where Koinonia is enabled
  • Card vote counts via the Trello Voting Power-Up
  • Your Trello API key and token, stored locally in your browser via Trello's plugin storage

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not store any data on our servers
  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking

How Your Data Is Used

Koinonia uses your Trello API credentials solely to read card positions and vote counts, and to reorder cards within lists you have explicitly selected. All operations happen directly between your browser and the Trello API.

Data Storage

Your API key and token are stored privately in Trello's own plugin data storage, scoped to your Trello account. Koinonia does not transmit or store these credentials anywhere else.
